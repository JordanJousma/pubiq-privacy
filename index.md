# PubIQ Privacy Policy

**Last updated:** March 12, 2026

## What PubIQ Does

PubIQ is a Chrome extension that adds research intelligence to PubMed search results, including journal metrics, NIH funding data, citation counts, and institution rankings.

## Data We Collect

**PubIQ does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data.**

Specifically:
- We do not collect your name, email, or any identifying information
- We do not track your browsing history
- We do not track which PubMed searches you perform
- We do not use cookies or analytics
- We do not sell any data to third parties
- We do not display advertisements

## Data That Stays on Your Device

PubIQ stores the following data locally in your browser only:
- Cached API responses (journal metrics, funding data) to reduce network requests
- Your extension preferences/settings

This data never leaves your browser and is automatically cleared when you uninstall the extension.

## External API Calls

To provide research intelligence, PubIQ makes requests to the following public APIs:

- **NCBI E-utilities** (eutils.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov) — to fetch paper metadata and grant information
- **NIH RePORTER** (api.reporter.nih.gov) — to fetch PI funding profiles
- **Europe PMC** (www.ebi.ac.uk) — to fetch citation counts

These are public government and academic APIs. PubIQ sends only PubMed IDs and author names to these services — no personal information about you.

## Permissions

PubIQ requests the following Chrome permissions:
- **activeTab** — to read PubMed page content and inject research intelligence
- **storage** — to cache API responses locally for performance

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected in the "Last updated" date above.
